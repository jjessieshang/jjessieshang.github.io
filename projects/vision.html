September 2017 - May 2022 / Founder


Skills: Leadership, Volunteering


The Problem
Youth from immigrant families face systematic challenges, a major one being feelings of isolation from their community.

My Solution
I created an accessible sports program where isolated youth can build connections. Over the course of four years, Vision Youth Sports earned the sponsorship of Calgary Immigration Services and 3 local schools and community centres to provide competitive volleyball training at no cost to 300+ youth.


Challenges
When the COVID-19 pandemic hit, I led a team of 3 coaches to pivot Vision Youth Sports to an online experience. We coordinated and executed online workout events with 75+ attendees each session and developed multifaceted training programmes to keep youth physically active at home.
