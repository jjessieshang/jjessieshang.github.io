audio biofeedback sensor

2022

Role: Software Engineer

Technologies: C, Arduino, MATLAB, Bluetooth, Circuit Design

Stakeholder: Tetra Society & Simon Cox Design Competition 2022

Problem: Prolonged involuntary muscle activation of the trapezius muscle creates back pain in patients with mobility impairments.

Solution:
We created a small, wearable device which detects muscle activation and provides audible feedback to the client's headset via Bluetooth
I established a pipeline to transfer signals from the arduino device, through a bluetooth module to be received by a computer, and directly passed into the MATLAB software for signal processing.
