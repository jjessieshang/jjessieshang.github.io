The Problem
A need for organization and automation of BC Cancer Labratory's day-to-day tasks

Our Solution
We worked closely with lab members to iteratively create a web-based lab management software. The app consists of four main compnents:

  • Form builder
  • Task Management board
  • Cost Center
  • Billing dashboard & Analytics

Billing dashboard & Analytics
I created a series of interactive cost analytics to increase visibility of billing trends across 300+ lab services. The aim was to help lab members easily identify their most valuable services, most frequent clients, and the breakdown of task completion by employees.
